About the Company

Key areas of activity of HYPROTEC LLC:

  • Innovation Center

    Provision of service operations using an automated online monitoring system for drilling fluid parameters installed directly on the rig.

  • Scientific and Technological Center
    • Independent laboratory testing of cement and drilling fluids, as well as fracturing fluids (FSA accreditation in accordance with GOST ISO/IEC 17025-2019).
    • Supervision and auditing of contractors, processes, and laboratories in drilling fluid and well cementing domains.
    • Expert conclusions and formulation of technical fluid recipes.
  • Professional Education Center

    Training and education programs on drilling and cementing fluids for industry specialists.

  • Trading House

    Supply of laboratory equipment with warranty and post-warranty service across Russia and abroad.

  • Service Center

    Commissioning, repair, maintenance, and calibration of laboratory equipment.

STC Accreditation

The laboratory in Novy Urengoy is accredited by the FSA under GOST ISO/IEC 17025-2019 — ensuring the accuracy and independence of all tests.

Laboratory Equipment

HYPROTEC LLC regularly supplies American, Russian, and Chinese laboratory equipment and spare parts to third-party companies, as well as for internal use. Our Scientific and Technological Center in Novy Urengoy hosts a large permanent testing laboratory, which has been conducting daily testing of drilling fluids, cement slurries, and fracturing fluids since 2020.

During the expansion of our own research and development capabilities, HYPROTEC faced the challenge of finding reliable manufacturers of professional-grade laboratory equipment. In solving this task, we encountered several key challenges:

  • Shifts in market landscape and reduced availability of Western equipment
  • Search for alternative solutions in local and allied markets
  • Assessment of equipment reliability and availability
