About us

Hyprotec's Core Business Divisions:

  • Innovation Center

    Real-time drilling fluid monitoring services based on our own automated systems installed directly on drilling rigs.

  • Science & Technology Center
    • Independent laboratory testing of cement slurries, drilling fluids, and fracturing fluids in an FSA-accredited lab.
    • Supervision & audits of contractors, processes, and labs for drilling fluids, solids control, and well cementing operations
    • Expert consulting and customized fluid formulation development
  • Professional Training Center

    Specialized training programs on drilling and cementing fluids

  • Supply Division

    Supply of laboratory equipment for drilling and cementing fluids, backed by warranty and post-warranty service internationally.

  • Service Center

    Commissioning, repair, maintenance, and calibration of laboratory equipment.

Key Accreditation

Our in-house Science & Technology Center in Novy Urengoy is accredited by the Federal Accreditation Service (FSA) under ISO/IEC 17025-2019.

Corporate Philosophy

Hyprotec is committed to engineering excellence, combining global best practices with stringent national standards to deliver unmatched quality.

Laboratory Equipment

HYPROTEC regularly supplies US, European, Russian, and Chinese laboratory equipment and spare parts to third-party companies, as well as for our own operations. Our Science and Technology Center houses a large permanent testing laboratory, where daily analyses of drilling fluids, cement slurries, and fracturing fluids have been conducted since 2020.

As we expanded, we faced several key challenges in sourcing reliable lab equipment manufacturers, including:

  • Shifts in market conditions and availability of equipment
  • Sourcing solutions from both domestic and friendly-country suppliers
  • Evaluating equipment reliability and supply chain stability
